Airports

Stuttgart Airport (STR) is 27 mi / 44 km (35 min drive) from the wedding venue.
STR is the closest airport to the wedding venue.

Munich Airport (MUC) is 120 mi / 195 km (2 hr 15 min drive) from the wedding venue.
Munich is a beautiful city to visit and there are direct flights from LAX to MUC.

Frankfurt Airport (FRA) is 150 mi / 240 km (2 hr 35 min drive) from the wedding venue.
Frankfurt is the most convenient and there are also direct flights from LAX to FRA.

Train Stations

The closest train station from the wedding venue is in Eislingen/Fils.
Larger train stations in the area are Goeppingen and Stuttgart Main Station.

If a pickup service from any of the mentioned hotels or trainstation is needed, please reach out to us.
